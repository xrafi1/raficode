Dil ki geheraion se — 2026
Meri pyaari Rifat,

Tumse bahut jyada pyar karta hun main. Itna ki batana mushkil hai — alfaaz chote pad jaate hain, aur dil mein jo bhar aata hai, woh kaagaz par nahi aa paata.

Par jo tumne mere saath kiya — woh bahut galat tha. Bahut zyada galat. Is tarah rulate ho mujhe, aur tumhe ehsas bhi nahi hota. Dard ko mein kahan le jaun, kise dikhaaun?

· · · ✦ · · ·

Par ek baat yaad rakhna — ek din khuda ke saamne jawab dena padega. Har cheez ka hisaab hoga wahan. Koi cheez chhupi nahi rahegi — na tumhari, na meri.
وَهُوَ عَلَىٰ كُلِّ شَيْءٍ قَدِيرٌ

Agar tum Allah ko sachchi maanti hoti... toh aisa nahi karte mere saath kabhi. Dil todna bhi ek gunah hai — aur woh gunah tumhare naam likhta ja raha hai kahin.

Andar se toot gaya hun bilkul. Lekin phir bhi — dua karta hun ki tum khush raho. Tumhari zindagi mein sukoon aaye, chahe mujhe milta na ho. Yahi sachchi mohabbat hoti hai.

Bas itna kehna tha — kash tumne samjha hota. Kash...

Tumhara hi rehega, — Tum jante ho kaun 💔
