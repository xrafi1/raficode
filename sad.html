Dekho Rifat, tumse bahut jyada pyar karta hun main. Itna ki batana mushkil hai.

Par jo tumne mere saath kiya — woh bahut galat tha. Bahut zyada galat.

Is tarah rulate ho mujhe, aur tumhe ehsas bhi nahi hota. Par ek din khuda ke saamne jawab dena padega tumhe. Har cheez ka hisaab hoga wahan.

Agar tum Allah ko maanti hoti... toh aisa nahi karte mere saath kabhi.

Ab main kya bataaun — jeene ka man hi nahi raha. Andar se toot gaya hun bilkul. Mann mein kitni cheezein aati hain... aisi cheezein jo sochni bhi nahi chahiye.

Bas itna kehna hai — kash tumne samjha hota.
